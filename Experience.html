As a partner in Because Video I improved my customer service, project management and sales skills. My encompassed all elements of the business – on-set production, post-production, working with clients and sales. I learned to be both a self-starter and a finisher. We worked with a wide variety of companies from start-ups to multi-nationals. The videos were geared at health professionals throughout Ireland and were needed within a very tight timeframe. I was grateful to have the opportunity to take on the work during the pandemic.
